What are horoscope:
A horoscope is an astrological chart or forecast that interprets the supposed influence of celestial bodies (such as the sun, moon, planets, and stars) on human life. It is based on the date, time, and location of a person's birth. The word "horoscope" comes from the Greek words hōra (meaning "time") and skopos (meaning "observer"), referring to observing the positions of celestial objects at a specific moment.
Horoscopes are often associated with the zodiac, which is divided into 12 signs, each corresponding to a specific period of the year. These signs are believed to influence personality traits, behaviors, and future events. Horoscopes can be found in newspapers, magazines, websites, and astrology apps, offering predictions related to love, career, health, and personal growth.
